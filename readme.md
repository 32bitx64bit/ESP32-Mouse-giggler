Just a small projected I decided to work on.

A nice little mouse giggler to keep your computer from falling asleep, especially helpful on corporate computers.

Specifically designed to run on : https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08VGST8LJ

How it works :
Connects via bluetooth, and moves the mouse back and fourth by a single pixel.

Meaning you can leave this on 24/7 without even noticing it. 

### Controls

- Press `A` to enable or disable the giggler
- Hold `A` for 5 seconds to clear saved Bluetooth bonds and reboot
- Hold `B` to open a 60 second pairing window

Once paired, the host should stay bonded until you clear bonds manually.
